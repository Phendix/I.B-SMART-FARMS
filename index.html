About I.B SMART FARMS

I.B SMART FARMS is an innovation-driven agricultural enterprise committed to modern, sustainable, and profitable farming systems. We combine practical production with capacity building to create opportunities across the agricultural value chain. From poultry and fish production to crop cultivation, garri processing, agro merchandise supply, and mentorship programs, we operate as a complete agribusiness ecosystem.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable agricultural production, value addition, and agribusiness development through innovation, training, and inclusive empowerment of youth and women.

Our Vision

To become a leading agricultural innovation and training hub in Nigeria, driving food security, economic growth, and community transformation.

Ali Ibrahim Musa

Founder & CEO

Meet The Visionary

Ali Ibrahim Musa is an agripreneur, agricultural innovator, and youth development advocate dedicated to transforming agriculture into a tool for sustainable food security, economic empowerment, and innovation in Nigeria. He is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of I.B SMART FARMS, a diversified agribusiness enterprise engaged in poultry production, fisheries, crop cultivation, agro-processing, agro merchandise supply, and agricultural training. Under his leadership, the farm operates as both a production enterprise and a platform for innovation, mentorship, and community impact. Ali is the visionary behind the NextGen Agriculture Program (NeGAP) — an initiative designed to equip students, youth, and women with practical agricultural skills, modern farming techniques, and entrepreneurship training to build profitable and sustainable agribusiness ventures.
